User Guide - How to Use the Geographical Information App

Welcome to the user guide for our Geographical Information App! This guide will walk you through the steps of using the app to explore cities and locations from all around the world.

Step 1: Enter City Name

To get started, type the name of the city you want to explore in the search bar at the top of the page.

Step 2: Click Search

After entering the city name, click the "Search" button to initiate the search process.

Step 3: Explore Geographical Information

Once the search is complete, you will see a card with detailed geographical information about the city you searched for. This information includes the city's name, state or administrative area, country, timezone, and more.

Step 4: Additional Information

The card also provides additional details such as Admin Area 2, Admin Area 3, City ID, and precise Longitude and Latitude coordinates.

Step 5: Explore Other Cities

You can explore information for other cities by scrolling down to the "Other Cities" section, where a table displays data for different cities.

Step 6: Start Your Journey

That's it! You are now ready to start your journey of discovery with our Geographical Information App. Explore the world and unlock fascinating details about cities near and far!

If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to contact our support team for help.
